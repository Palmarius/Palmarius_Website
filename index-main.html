PALMARIUS

Palmarius 2K16 is the National level Techfest hosted by Sahrdaya College of Engineering & Technology in connection with Techtop Innovation challenge. TechTop is annually held to identify, promote and celebrate outstanding engineering talents with innovative ideas . Come and experience the joint effort of 6 departments; ECE, CSE,CE, EEE, BME, BTE of Sahrdaya College of Engineering & Technology; as we take you through a technical extravaganza with multi-discipline events all through 5th & 6th August 2016.
